Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Call Center Management App (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects the information from users (“you”) in connection with the app.

Information We Collect

To provide full functionality, the app requires access to certain data and system resources:

  • SMS Messages: Read, send, and receive SMS to log communication and support messaging features (READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS, SEND_SMS).
  • Call Logs & Phone State: Access and manage call logs, phone state, and outgoing/incoming calls to monitor and manage call center operations (READ_CALL_LOG, READ_PHONE_STATE, PHONE_STATE, NEW_OUTGOING_CALL, CALL_PHONE, ANSWER_PHONE_CALLS, MANAGE_OWN_CALLS).
  • Contacts: Read contacts to allow easier call management (READ_CONTACTS).
  • Audio & Microphone: Record calls for quality monitoring (RECORD_AUDIO, MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS).
  • Device & System Settings: Wake lock, disable keyguard, request battery optimizations to ensure uninterrupted background operations (DISABLE_KEYGUARD, WAKE_LOCK, REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS, FOREGROUND_SERVICE, RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED).
  • Internet & Network: Access the internet and network state to sync data and manage communications (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE).

How We Use Your Data

  • Manage and log incoming and outgoing calls for call center efficiency.
  • Record calls and messages where legally permitted for training and quality assurance.
  • Synchronize data with our server to maintain call history and user activity logs.
  • Enable features that require real-time access to phone and network state.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data. All sensitive information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. Your call recordings and messages are never shared with third parties without explicit consent.

User Control & Consent

By using this app, you consent to the permissions requested and data processing as described. You can revoke certain permissions through your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through the app or other communication channels.
